Heart Disease Dataset

Exploratory Data Analysis

  • Here's a snapshot of what the data looks like
  • Target column indicates the presence of heart disease from 0 - 4
  • Zero is no presence of heart disease and 4 is severe heart disease
  • 303 rows and 14 columns
  • Basic statistics of the dataset
  • Average age of patient is 54
  • No missing values in the dataset
  • No duplicate values in the dataset
  • Renaming patient number column for ease of use
  • Identifing correlations with a heatmap
  • Futher identification of correlations with a pairplot
  • Positive correlation between resting blood pressure and age
  • As patient age rises so does resting blood pressure
  • Negative correlation between maximum heart rate and age
  • As patient age rises the maximum heart rate decreases
  • Prediction - naive bayes classifier
  • Ratio of 80% - 20% for train and test data
  • Consistently acheiving 50.82% accuarcy rate
